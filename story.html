An Unfamiliar Village

As a wandering Nomad in the tibetan mountains, you have been to countless villages and gurus to find peace.

However, several months into your journey, you find a small temple tucked between two unfamiliar, radiating mountains.

A large voice booms down from above and says, "ENTER YOUNG ONE. FOR I WILL TELL YOU YOUR FORTUNE!"

Without hesitation, you decide to enter the meditation room.
